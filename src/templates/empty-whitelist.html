Allowed Senders
Your allowed senders list is currently empty.

Use the Allowed Senders list to create a list of senders who are protected from any antispam scanning or junk mail filtering. Messages from senders on your Allowed Senders list are always sent to your Inbox.

NOTE: Senders who are in your Address Book are also protected from antispam and junk mail scanning. Move people to your Address Book and complete their contact information if you intend to correspond with them. This will allow address auto-completion and other advanced features to work on these addresses. Use the Allowed Senders only for people whom you do not want to have in your regular Address Book.

Adding to Allowed Senders List

There are two ways to add to your allowed senders list:

  • hit the not spam link for messages that ended up in your spam folder
  • enter a new email address by going to Address>New Address to Folder menu, you only need to enter the email address as all other fields are optional

To remove senders from your Allowed Senders list, highlight the sender's e-mail address or name and hit the Delete key on your keyboard. You can also access this option from the popup menu of the Allowed Senders window. That sender disappears from your Allowed Senders list and is now subject to antispam scanning and junk mail filtering.

Upgrading Contacts from Allowed Senders to Address Book

View the Allowed Senders list and then Drag & Drop the contacts to your regular address book. You can then edit the contact to include additional information such as their phone numbers and addresses.

Sharing Allowed Senders List

Allowed Senders List, just like your Address Books can be shared. To share the Allowed Senders List with other users, select File->Invite to Share Address Book from the window top menu. Select the Customize Sharing Properties option, then press the Add button to add other users or groups.
