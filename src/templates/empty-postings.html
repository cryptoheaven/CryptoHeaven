CryptoHeaven allows you to have a discussion with a single or multiple partners in a secure environment.

Anyone on your contact list can be invited to participate. To invite people to participate in your discussion, select Folder Properties->Sharing from the folder pop-up menu. Make sure the Customize sharing properties option is selected, then press the Add button to invite others.

Your messages may include file attachments.
