Security & Privacy

In contrast to most other security providers, all your messages and files get encrypted on your computer, so that no one - including the employees at CryptoHeaven Corp. - can access your private email and files.


Encrypted Online Storage

For enhanced security, all messages and files reside exclusively on encrypted online storage, so that absolutely nothing is stored on your local computers.
