Secure Instant Messaging

CryptoHeaven Secure Instant Messaging encrypts your Instant Messages end-to-end with the same level of encryption as all other CryptoHeaven components. Your messages are encrypted while in transit and while in storage. Absolutely nothing is stored on your or your recipient's computer.

Tips
Create a group chat session by selecting multiple contacts or a group from your Contact List.

Add people to an existing chat session by selecting Invite to the Conversation from the chat pop-up menu.

Change chat log history retention from the default of 100 messages by going to the folder properties menu.
