Uploading

You can use drag & drop to upload files and folders.


Sharing

By default CryptoHeaven File Folders are private, but you can easily share them with people on your Contact List or by sending a link via email.


Security & Privacy

In contrast to most other security providers, all your email and files get encrypted on your computer, so that no one - including the employees at CryptoHeaven Corp. - can access your private email and files.


Encrypted Online Storage

For enhanced security, all messages and files reside exclusively on encrypted online storage, so that absolutely nothing is stored on your local computers.
