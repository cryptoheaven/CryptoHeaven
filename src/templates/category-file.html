Secure File Folders
CryptoHeaven is an Internet tool that provides an easy way for mobile professionals to access, store, backup and share documents with workgroup members located anywhere in the world. Only people whom you have explicitly granted permission to will be able to access files in a shared folder.

Simply create your file folders, upload or drag and drop files onto it and invite other to access and share the information.

Right click an item to see a popup menu and more context sensitive actions.
Tips
By default CryptoHeaven File Folders are PRIVATE, but you can securely share them (help) with people on your Contact List.

Upload files with simple drag & drop or by clicking on theicon located on the toolbar.

To modify folder share properties right click on the desired folder to get the popup menu, click Share Folder.
