Sending Secure Email

Any email sent to any person on your Contact List or to any other CryptoHeaven user is always encrypted. Encryption is transparent and does not require any further input from you. All file attachments are also encrypted.

Individual Message Encryption

Any email sent to any regular email account can be individually encrypted with a "Question and Answer". To send an individually encrypted message compose the message and hit the send button. You will be asked if you want to encrypt the message with a question and answer.

Anonymous Email

None of the messages sent from your account contain any personally identifiable information or your IP address.

Address Book

Don't forget to add people to your Address Book. People can be added individually or imported from external sources. Anyone in your Address Book is exempt from spam filtering and their messages will never end up in your Spam folder.
